About

Our Mission

The University of Maryland Inclusive Growth (IG) Initiative is an organization that has combined all the resources and programs that the University offers to the community surrounding campus. Our mission is to serve local communities by providing resources and assisting with their needs (If we are not meeting your needs, please let us know, please contact this email). The goal is to provide an inventory of resources already available and make them accessible for the community to utilize.

The (IG) Initiative aims to support inclusive economic impact by providing opportunities in and around the College Park area. Our initiatives include career development, family and employment, entrepreneurship, healthcare, sustainable housing, and transportation resources for community members.

Our Community

Currently, the University of Maryland has over 120 different resources and projects aimed at our community stakeholders. These resources do not mean anything unless we are able to provide them to communities in need or who are just looking for some additional support. UMD IG hopes to begin accomplishing this by making the programs more accessible.
