Career Services

To help students and alumni discover their talents and interests, choose an academic major, explore industries and career options, and create an effective job search strategy, we offer career coaching, self-assessment, online resume and cover letter development, and assistance with networking and interviewing. Through information sessions, a full on-campus interviewing program, and involvement in job and industry fairs, seminars, and panels, we also assist employers promote their businesses on campus.
